Privacy Policy for DataCop

Effective Date: May 2026

DataCop ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices.

1. Data Collection

DataCop follows a Zero-Data Collection policy. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personally identifiable information, browsing history, or user data to any external servers.

2. Use of Permissions

The Extension uses the following permissions solely for its core functionality:

  • Host Permission (): Used to detect and dismiss cookie banners on websites you visit.
  • Scripting: Used to inject local logic to restore page scrolling after a banner is hidden.
  • Storage: Used to save your local "Heavy Duty Mode" preference on your device.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect it in the first place.

4. Contact

For questions, contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support dashboard.
